What is "Z"COIN?

The digital real estate market of a new generation, that will unite B2B, B2C and C2C in the single world-wide or regional network. "Z" COIN is a multifunctioning, multilevel platform based on the Blockchain technology with the use of iDeals (the next generation of smart contracts).

We use the Blockchain technology for:
• Securing storage of real estate objects, subjects, deals and financial transactions;
• Survivability;
• Traceability;
• Operational and Business Continuity;
• Investment risks minimization.

"Z"COIN Platform™ has an adaptive learning information security system based on the most complete and up-to-date threat matrix, on an expanding set of rules and conditions for process verification at all stages of the circulation of information on the "Z"COIN Platform (Security Gateways), and also on the analysis of the hacker attacks.

Security system for "Z"COIN Platform™ is based on the following standards:
• ISO 27002;
• PCI DSS;
• The Second Payment Services Directive (PSD2);
• Multifactor (3+) Authentication;
• Domestic security regulations.

The "Z"COIN© is the equivalent of money, which performs the following two functions: measure of value and medium of exchange.

Advantages of using own cryptocurrency "Z"COIN© on our Platform are allowing to overcome some domestic and international barriers in the cooperative global environment and to have the ability to simplify and optimize transactions

"Z"COIN Platform™ contracts and deals are much wider than well-known Smart Contracts concept of existing implementations (ex. Ethereum and Bitcoin).

iDeals™- next generation of smart-contracts. It is based on unique concept of Business Process engine and “Z”COIN Intelligent Deals Engine using our own BPEL named “Z”COIN Intelligent Deals.
